About Us

A privately owned and run business, theeventslounge.com, has been serving many private and corporate clients in the and around the area for many years. We only use the best quality equipment and offer a personable service to cater for all our clients needs.

We have a full £10M public liability insurance and all equipment is PAT certified. As event planning goes, our team takes thinking outside the box to a new level. It's a matter of mixing ideas up, working collectively, and finding inspiration. To say we focus on the little things and details couldn't be truer, as it's the details that matter and make an event memorable.
